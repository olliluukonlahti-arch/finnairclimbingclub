About the club

The club welcomes beginners and experienced climbers alike. The goal is not performance. The goal is to enjoy climbing, build skills, and connect with colleagues in a relaxed and supportive atmosphere.

What we do

We climb indoors and outdoors, share tips, and learn together with a strong focus on safety.

  • Indoor sessions and occasional outdoor trips
  • Basics and safe belaying practices
  • Technique tips and training ideas
  • Friendly vibe, no pressure

Principles

We aim to provide an easy way to enjoy climbing, improve personal skills, and practice safely. More experienced climbers are happy to share tips, and newcomers can easily feel part of the group.

Communication and next steps

After you join, you will receive information about upcoming events and practical details. You can also share your wishes for the club when signing up.
